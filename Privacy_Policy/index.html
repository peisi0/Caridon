Privacy Policy for Caridon University

Effective Date: 22/4/2025

Caridon University (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of students, faculty, staff, and visitors who interact with our website (https://peisi0.github.io/Caridon) and related services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other identifiers when you apply for programs, request information, or register for events.
    • Student records, academic history, and financial details (for enrolled students).
  • Non-Personal Information:
    • Browser type, IP address, device information, and usage data collected via cookies and analytics tools.
    • Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot identify individuals.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process admissions, enrollment, and academic services.
  • Communicate with you about university updates, events, or administrative matters.
  • Improve website functionality, services, and user experience.
  • Conduct research and analysis (using anonymized data where possible).
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., FERPA, GDPR, or other regulations).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties assisting with IT, payment processing, or analytics (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights.
  • Academic Partners: For collaborative programs or research (with consent where applicable).
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or reorganizations.

4. Data Security

We implement safeguards such as encryption, SSL protocols, and access controls to protect your data. However, no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable).
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@caridon.edu.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You can adjust browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit website functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external sites (e.g., payment portals, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

We comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
